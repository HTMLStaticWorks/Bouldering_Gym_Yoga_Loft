OUR STORY

Building a community of strength, balance, and friendship since 2018.

HOW IT STARTED

What began as a small group of climbers in a converted warehouse has grown into the city's premier destination for holistic fitness.

Founded by Sarah and Mark, two passionate climbers who felt the city lacked a space that combined the intensity of bouldering with the mindfulness of yoga. They envisioned a place where physical limits are tested and mental focus is cultivated under one roof.
